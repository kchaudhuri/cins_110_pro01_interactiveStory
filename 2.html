Tale of fortunes !!

Jack and Prince Charles meet at the palace after the prince saves Jack from an angry guard. After discussing their different lives, the two exchange clothes for fun and discover that they look exactly alike. When the prince rushes out to scold the guards in Jack's rags, he is not allowed back in and the switch becomes permanent.
